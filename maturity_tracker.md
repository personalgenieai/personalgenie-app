# Genie Maturity Tracker — Domain Design

Each domain has 5 signal vectors scored 0–4. Total = 20 points per domain.
Displayed as a phase label + percentage to user. Gaps surface as specific asks.

---

## Domain 1 — Relationships (per person)

| Vector | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Identity** | Unknown | Name only | Name + role | Role + context | Confirmed + nuanced |
| **Emotional Valence** | None | Rough sentiment | Clear feeling | How you feel + why | Confirmed + evolving |
| **Contact Pattern** | None | Frequency known | Last contact known | Pattern over time | Drift detected + flagged |
| **Shared History** | None | 1–2 facts | Some memories | Rich history | Specific moments confirmed |
| **Your Goal** | None | Vague intent | Direction stated | Specific goal set | Active + being tracked |

**Phases:** Stranger (0–24%) → Acquaintance (25–49%) → Known (50–74%) → Close (75–89%) → Understood (90–100%)

---

## Domain 2 — Physical Health

| Vector | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Baseline** | Unknown | Self-reported level | Observed from data | Validated baseline | Tracked over time |
| **Routine** | None | Rough frequency | Schedule known | Consistency pattern | Gaps + trends identified |
| **Goals** | None | Vague aspiration | Specific goal | Measurable target | Active tracking |
| **Professional Support** | None | Has trainer/coach | Trainer known | Trainer notes ingested | Ongoing signal |
| **Performance Data** | None | 1–2 data points | Some PRs/metrics | Trend established | Benchmarks set |

**Phases:** Blank (0–24%) → Signal (25–49%) → Tracked (50–74%) → Active (75–89%) → Optimized (90–100%)

---

## Domain 3 — Nutrition

| Vector | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Diet Type** | Unknown | Rough preferences | Restrictions known | Cuisine + habit pattern | Confirmed hard preferences |
| **Meal Pattern** | None | Rough timing | When + how often | Daily rhythm clear | Deviations tracked |
| **Relationship with Food** | None | Casual signal | Some emotional context | Pattern identified | Nuanced + confirmed |
| **Goals** | None | Vague ("eat better") | Specific goal | Measurable target | Active + logged |
| **Problem Areas** | None | One flagged | Two flagged | Patterns identified | Being actively addressed |

**Phases:** Dark (0–24%) → Surface (25–49%) → Pattern (50–74%) → Detailed (75–89%) → Calibrated (90–100%)

---

## Domain 4 — Exercise

| Vector | 0 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Types** | Unknown | One type known | 2–3 types | Full picture | New types tracked as added |
| **Frequency** | None | Rough estimate | Weekly schedule | Session-level detail | Streak + consistency tracked |
| **Intensity** | None | Light/moderate/hard | RPE or equivalent | HR / performance data | Training load understood |
| **Goals** | None | "Get fitter" | Specific goal | Measurable target | Periodized toward it |
| **Recovery** | None | None | Sleep + rest rough | Recovery habits known | Active recovery modeled |

**Phases:** Cold (0–24%) → Warming (25–49%) → Active (50–74%) → Training (75–89%) → Performing (90–100%)

---

## How Gaps Surface

The Genie references maturity gaps naturally in sessions — never as a form:

> "I know your training schedule well but I'm almost blind on your nutrition.
>  What did you eat yesterday? Just roughly — I'm building a picture."

> "Your relationship with TJ is the one I understand best.
>  Barry I know less about — you mentioned him a few times but I don't have a sense
>  of what that friendship actually means to you."

Gap completion = the user's primary engagement loop in month one.
Target: all 4 domains at 50%+ by end of week 2, 75%+ by end of month 1.
